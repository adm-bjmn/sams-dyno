About

For the past three decades Sam’s Dyno Centre has been working tirelessly to perfect the art of motorcycle performance. Founded by former Speedway Champion Sam Ermolenko in 1996, the shop originally served to allow Sam to develop and test his own bikes, a practice which contributed significantly to his Speedway success. Sam decided to open the shop to the public in 2001 allowing him to work with top racers and teams from all across the globe. As a DYNOJET authorised dealer Sam’s Dyno Centre has the necessary skills and expertise to fine tune your motorcycle and achieve its optimum performance while our in-house mechanics and engineers make us the one stop shop for all motorcycle enthusiasts and one of South Derbyshire’s best kept secrets.
